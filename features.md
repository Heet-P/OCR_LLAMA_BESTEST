# Techathon: OCR & Form Intelligence

### Key Features
- **Advanced AI OCR**: Leverages high-precision vision models for accurate text extraction from multi-page PDFs and images.
- **Interactive Search**: Real-time visual highlighting and coordinate mapping for instant text location within documents.
- **Automated Workflows**: Streamlines manual form-filling with pixel-perfect data placement and background processing.
- **Scalable Infrastructure**: Built with a robust FastAPI backend and Supabase for efficient document management and storage.

### The Solution
Our platform bridges the digital gap by automating the extraction and processing of physical documents, eliminating manual errors and saving hours of administrative work.
